Red Fox



All about the Red Fox

Facts about Red Foxes

  1. Super Senses: Red foxes possess an exceptional sense of hearing, with the ability to hear low-frequency sounds like a rodent digging underground or a mouse squeaking 100 feet away
  2. Vocal Range: They can produce more than 40 different vocalizations, ranging from barks and whines to a loud, high-pitched scream used during the winter mating season.
  3. Nocturnal Hunters: While they can be active during the day, they prefer to hunt at twilight and overnight.

Preys of the Red Fox

  • Mice and Voles
  • Rabbits and Hares
  • Squirrels, chipmunks
  • Red Fox
  • Gray Wolf
