All about Tigers

Facts about Tigers

  1. Tigers hide the carcasses of their prey.
  2. In the wild, there are only about 3,100 tigers remaining.
  3. The average lifespan of a tiger is about 10 to 15 years.

Preys of the Tiger

  • Wild boar
  • deer species (sambar and chital)
  • water buffalo
  • gaur
  • hares, porcupines
